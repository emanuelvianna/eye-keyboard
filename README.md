# eye-keyboard
Extending webcam-eyetracker for use a keyboard with pupils

The keyboard layout is shown below:

|       | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 | 9 |
|-------|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | A | B | C | D | E | F | G | H | I |
| **2** | J | K | L | M | N | O | P | Q | R |
| **3** | S | T | U | V | X | W | Y | Z |   |
